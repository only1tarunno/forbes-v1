The first step is to set up a secure crypto wallet to store your Doge Rush ($DR) tokens. The Doge Rush team recommends using MetaMask for desktop users or Trust Wallet for mobile users, as these wallets support ERC-20 tokens.

To set up MetaMask, visit the official website. and download the application. Follow the instructions to create a password and set up your wallet.

Step 2 – Obtain ETH or USDT

To participate in the Doge Rush presale, you will need either Ethereum (ETH) or Tether (USDT) tokens. These can be obtained from reputable cryptocurrency exchanges. Once you have purchased ETH or USDT, transfer them to your crypto wallet created in Step 1.

Alternatively, the Doge Rush presale platform offers a built-in widget that allows users to purchase $DR tokens using a credit/debit card. However, please note that this option may involve additional fees through the Wert digital payment solution that powers the widget.

Step 3 – Link Wallet to Presale Platform

Visit the Doge Rush website and click on the "Connect Wallet" button. A pop-up box will appear, prompting you to choose your wallet provider. Follow the on-screen instructions to link your crypto wallet to the Doge Rush presale platform.

Step 4 – Buy $DR Tokens

After linking your wallet, an order box will be displayed on the screen. Here, you can choose the digital currency you wish to use (ETH or USDT) and specify the quantity of $DR tokens you want to purchase. Please note that the minimum purchase amount is $100.

Step 5 – Claim $DR Tokens

Once the Doge Rush presale concludes, you can claim your purchased $DR tokens from the official website. Unlike some projects, there is no vesting period, meaning that all tokens will become immediately available for you to access and manage.
